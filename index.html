The Philosophy

Quality in thought, dedication in every deed , and customer focus in every product, is the philosophy That guides this successful company in today’s Competitive World. Among stiff competition in its class, THEE PERFECT COOLING SYSTEMS (PCS) started production in the prime Sidco Industrial Estate located in the main area of Salem.

The Beginning

Thee Perfect Cooling System commenced its operations of manufacturing Radiators and Oil Coolers In the year 2010 under the brand name of “PERFECT COOL RADIATORS” . This Company was ably Managed by its dynamic and Industrious founder Mr. K. Parthiban . He has about 35 years Experience in marketing Radiators . In order to offer his valuable customers with more durable Radiators , Oil Coolers and Heat Exchangers, he embarked upon this venture

The Technology

PCR in order to maintain Sturdier quality in all its products, adopts only the latest technology that is Available in the industry. It uses the updated German Technology to get more heat transfer and more heat dissipation for which it is producing Flat oval lock seemed Radiator Brass Tubes and two types of Radiator Copper Fins, One is pierced out Fins with bulb type end folding and another is megard type Fins with louvers. We are developing Aluminum finned tubes by extrusion processes. Finned Tubes are used in all sectors asking for an efficient cooling.
